How to use this website?

This website provides a gallery of AI-generated images and videos with their corresponding prompts. You can browse through different categories like Images, Videos, and Trending. The trending section is always open by default. Use the Help and Contact buttons in the top navigation to get more information or to contact us.

How to copy a prompt?

To copy a prompt, simply click the "Copy Prompt" button located at the bottom of each card. A confirmation message will appear at the bottom of the screen when the prompt has been successfully copied.

What is Dark Mode?

You can toggle between light and dark themes using the circular switch in the top right corner. Dark mode makes the website easier on the eyes in low-light conditions.
